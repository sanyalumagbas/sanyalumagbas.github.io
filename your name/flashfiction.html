Mirror Me

I don't know what, but there's this part of me that keeps yearning for something. I tend to question myself, Is it something unattainable? Why is it that every breath I take feels so insincere?

When it comes to these unwanted thoughts, I usually go to the mirror and stare at the self I'm seeing. I love feeling like I'm a spectator while she's the one living the life I'm tired of living in.

No, I'm not trying to die. I'm not too keen on living, either. I just wanted to know, will there be something in my life that will make me realize how thankful I should be?
